My name is Ketan Shukla and I live in San Diego, CA.

I've been a software developer primarily in Microsoft technologies since the mid 90s and went for a career change in mid 2000s. I did all my work in VB.net, C# and mostly .NET when it came out in 2000.

In 2006, I had a change of heart and quit software development because web programming was so boring due to lack of sophisticated and exciting front end technologies, that I lost interest in coding completely.

Now I see what HTML5 and the various Javascript frameworks can do on the front end, along with how easy it is to create websites with templates such as Bootstrap and then combine that with Ruby on Rails and all of a sudden my passion to return to the arena has really sky rocketed.

My passion for software development has risen again and this time around I want to be an absolute expert in Full Stack development, especially in Ruby on Rails, which I find way more appealing as a programming language than C# or anything in .NET.

I am coming back to the software engineering industry after a TEN YEAR break and so therefore I thought to myself that the best and wisest way to enter back into this arena where I am now facing so much competition from people younger than me is to put myself through an intensely rigorous program, preferably guided by a mentor who knows exactly what is needed and required by people looking for RoR and Full Stack jobs and is themselves a person of many years of experience in the industry.

So after doing a lot of research, I came across Bloc and looked through their curriculum online and thought to myself that this is probably what I need. I spoke to the student counselors and registered. Then it came to choosing a mentor, you came recommended, and for the reasons mentioned above, I read your profile and thought to myself that you would be the perfect fit for me.

I'm currently registered for the RoR course, but really interested in the Full Stack program. That's what I'm really interested in, but in the meantime I also want to be an expert in RoR.
