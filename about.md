I have been in the software industry for the past 20 years fulfilling various roles such as developer, tester, architect, technical lead and have also managed teams.

In the early 90s I had primarily been involved with Microsoft technologies in creating desktop applications, websites, middle tier business logic and database programming including writing SQL scripts, automated data migration jobs.

In 2006, I transitioned into Internet Marketing where I was involved with Network Marketing, CPA (Cost Per Action) businesses, Affiliate Marketing (with ClickBank and other affiliates).

For the past year, my passion as a developer has rekindled and I've become very enthusiastic about the Ruby language and the Rails framework architecture. I discovered how much easier it is to do website development with Ruby on Rails and have created a few projects to demonstrate my abilities as seen on this site.
